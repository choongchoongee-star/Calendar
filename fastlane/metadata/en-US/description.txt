Chaeuda is a shared calendar app for managing schedules with family, partners, and friends.

Key Features:
- Multiple calendars with sharing — invite anyone with a simple link
- Cloud sync — access your schedule from anywhere in real time
- iOS widgets — see today's events at a glance on your home screen
- Recurring events — daily, weekly, and yearly repeats
- Korean public holidays displayed automatically
- Push notifications — get reminded 30 minutes before events
- Guest mode — start using immediately without signing in

Sign in with Google or Apple and start filling your calendar together.
